Extreme DDoS Defense (XD3)

Two Six Labs / Arlington, VA Jan 2018 - Present

I currently work as a front-end engineer for the DARPA Extreme DDoS Defense (XD3) program and focus on the visualization of complex network data and UI/Interaction design as a whole. I work with a diverse team of data scientists and engineers to solve cybersecurity challenges using machine learning and predictive analytics.

Two Six Labs is a government contractor working with the Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA) and the Department of Defense (DoD). The XD3 program’s goal is to detect and mitigate DDoS cybersecurity attacks with a focus on the detection of low-volume DDoS attacks. As a front-end engineer, I’m working to visualize network traffic from simulated attacks to allow data scientists to better understand and analyze the structure of both attacks and mitigation efforts.
